Platform overview

About

A compact Flask and SQLite application for county-level disease outbreak risk prediction.

01

What it does

The system reads the provided health dataset, scores county-level outbreak risk, explains the strongest risk drivers, and saves predictions.

02

Built with

  • Flask routes and Jinja templates
  • CSV dataset loading
  • SQLite prediction history
  • Responsive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
03

Scoring method

The score combines vaccination gap, social vulnerability, density, uninsured rate, chronic disease, poverty, housing, and environmental exposure indicators.
